Additions

 

If you're outgrowing your home, it's tempting to sell the house and buy a bigger one. This could be a financial mistake! Selling, obtaining, and moving away to a new home is expensive and heartbreaking. Realtor fees, closing costs, higher taxes, and a higher mortgage are just the beginning. Your new home will need landscaping, patios, patio covers, garage door openers and other add-ons once you move in. Instead, invest in your current home by adding a room. We will wow you with a variety of room additions whether it's on the ground floor or a second story. Dream up the comfort of a new master bedroom. Is a billiard room more your style? Allow us to help you extend your home with a new room addition.

  

Before running out and interviewing a realtor, consider adding on to your home instead. Sound too costly? It may be less expensive in the long run. Remember that you must pay the realtor a large percentage of the selling price along with pay the closing costs. In addition, you must buy a bigger home which means a larger and more expensive house payment and higher property taxes. New homes come with big expenses. You'll find yourself at the area home improvement store many Saturdays in a row getting your house livable. You'll need window coverings, landscaping, and dozens of extras you hadn't thought about before moving. You could keep your existing home and add on to it. Additions are a fabulous way to add square footage and increase your home's value at once. When the time eventually comes to sell your home, your investment will have paid off. In the meantime, you will get to treasure the additional space and amenities.

Our goal is that the work we do for you add value to your home.
